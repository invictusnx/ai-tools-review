What I Tested

  • Daily search: News, weather, sports scores, stock prices — the quick queries you'd normally Google
  • Deep research: Asked it to write a report on the EU AI Act's impact on UK startups
  • Fact-checking: Verified claims from news articles and social media posts
  • Academic research: Literature review on transformer architecture advancements

Pros & Cons

✓ Pros

  • Best-in-class AI search with real citations
  • Pro Search handles complex multi-step research
  • Deep Research generates genuine report-quality output
  • Clean, fast interface with good source transparency

✗ Cons

  • Free tier is very limited (5 Pro searches/day)
  • Weak for creative or conversational tasks
  • Local search lags behind Google
  • Deep Research is slow (2-5 minutes per report)

🏆 Final Verdict

8.5
/ 10

Perplexity is the best AI search tool for anyone who does research-heavy work — students, journalists, analysts, or curious people. Pro Search and Deep Research are genuinely useful tools that save hours. If your primary need is conversation or creativity, ChatGPT is a better fit. But for finding, synthesising, and citing information, Perplexity leads.
