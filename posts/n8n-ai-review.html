What I Tested

  • Web research agent: Scrape a topic, summarise with AI, save to Google Sheets, and email the report
  • Slack triage bot: Read incoming Slack messages, classify intent, route to the right channel or person
  • Data enrichment: Take a CSV of company names, enrich with web data, and update a CRM
  • Multi-branch automation: Build a conditional workflow that branches based on AI classification results

Pros & Cons

✓ Pros

  • 400+ integrations out of the box
  • Self-hostable (data privacy)
  • Visual builder — no coding needed
  • Structured AI output parsing
  • Active open-source community

✗ Cons

  • Free cloud tier is limited
  • Complex workflows get visually messy
  • No built-in multi-agent orchestration
  • Self-hosting requires server management

Pricing

Free self-hosted (unlimited). Cloud plans start at $20/month for 5K workflow executions. Pro at $50/month for 25K executions. Enterprise pricing is custom. The AI node uses your own API keys for LLM calls, so AI costs are separate.

Alternatives

  • Zapier Central — Easier to set up, fewer integrations, more expensive
  • Make (Integromat) — Good visual builder, weaker AI features
  • CrewAI — Better for multi-agent orchestration, code-focused
