About This Site

AI Tools Review is a independent site that tests and reviews AI tools hands-on. No sponsored reviews, no fluff — just honest takes on what actually works and what doesn't.

Every tool is tested in real usage scenarios before it gets reviewed. We prioritise tools that are accessible (free or affordable), genuinely useful, and actively maintained.

Why Trust Us?

We don't accept payment for positive reviews. If a tool is bad, we say so. Affiliate links are clearly marked — clicking them costs you nothing extra but helps keep this site running.

What We Cover

  • LLMs & AI Chatbots (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, DeepSeek)
  • AI Coding Tools (Cursor, Copilot, Claude Code, Bolt.new)
  • AI Search & Research (Perplexity, You.com, Consensus)
  • AI Design & Creative (Midjourney, Canva AI, Figma AI)
  • AI Productivity & Automation
  • New tools as they launch
