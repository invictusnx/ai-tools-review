ABOUT THIS SITE

AI Tools Review is exactly what it sounds like — honest, hands-on reviews of AI tools. No sponsored fluff, no vendor-soaked "reviews" written from a press release. Every tool on this site has been installed, tested, and put through real-world workflows before we publish a verdict.

Why This Exists

The AI landscape moves fast. Every week there's a new tool claiming to revolutionise how you code, write, design, or research. Most reviews are either (a) surface-level listicles written by someone who barely touched the product, or (b) official marketing content dressed up as journalism. This site is neither.

How We Review

We pick a tool, use it for real work, and write what we find. Each review covers:

  • What it actually does — clear, jargon-free explanation
  • What we tested — specific tasks and workflows
  • Performance breakdown — star ratings on the features that matter
  • Pros and cons — honest trade-offs, not just a feature list
  • Pricing — what you actually pay, including gotchas
  • Alternatives — when you should pick something else
