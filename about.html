SCORING

How ratings work.

Scores are not random stars. They are a quick verdict on whether the tool is genuinely useful after hands-on testing.

9–10Excellent — a best-in-class tool worth trying first.
7–8.9Strong — useful, but with trade-offs or better fits for certain users.
5–6.9Mixed — interesting, but not an easy recommendation for most people.
Below 5Weak — too limited, unreliable, overpriced, or not worth the workflow switch.
